About

Camel Cases is a collection of articles and code snippets written by me, Daniel Sundy. What qualifies me to publish things on the internet? Nothing formal (unless you count the English degree that’s rolled up in a drawer in my parents’ basement), but I spend most of my day wading through HTML, CSS and JavaSript at Helios Design Labs in Toronto, Canada. I’m hoping that I can give back a little bit to the design and development community by passing on some of the solutions I find.
